About Me

Hi!

I'm a sophmore at the Milwaukee School of Engineering studying Computer Science.
My journey with technology started when I got my first computer at age 10. Then in middle school I started learning HTML, JavaScript, and CSS to make simple games with my friend. In high school I took the AP computer science courses and learned how to make full stack web applications and java applications. In my senior year I accepted an internship at Kaukauna Utilities and the City of Kaukauna in Information Technology. Throughout the internship I learned about server administration, enterprise networking, and government cybersecurity standards. After my internship I started at MSOE and continued developing my skills with computers. During my first year I attended the school career fair and networked with companies. After interviewing with several companies I accepted an internship with Cognex corporation. At Cognex I became a software engineering intern and learned what it was like to develop software in the industry. I'm still learning and growing every day as a software engineer and I'm excited to see where my career takes me.
